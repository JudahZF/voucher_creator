CSV Format Guide

Your CSV file should contain voucher codes in the first column:

# This is a comment line
VOUCHER001
VOUCHER002
VOUCHER003
# Comments are ignored
VOUCHER004

Tip: Lines starting with # are treated as comments and will be ignored.
