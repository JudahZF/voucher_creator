CSV Format Requirements

Your CSV file should have voucher codes in the first column. Here's an example:

# WiFi Voucher Codes
# Lines starting with # are ignored
voucher_code
VOUCHER001
VOUCHER002
VOUCHER003
# Guest access codes
ABC123DEF
XYZ789GHI
Tips: Headers are optional. Lines starting with # are treated as comments and ignored. This allows you to organize your voucher codes with descriptions.
